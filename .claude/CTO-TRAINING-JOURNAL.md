## Key Insights for Publication

### What Makes This Training Effective

1. **Real Project:** Production-quality stakes, not toy examples
2. **Complementary Strengths:** AI handles detail, human handles strategy
3. **Iterative Process:** Risk analysis until no serious gaps
4. **Trust in Intuition:** "Don't merge yet" was correct
5. **Experience Integration:** Medical device background informs decisions
6. **Meta-cognition:** Aware of own growth, documenting it

### The "Force Multiplier" Effect

Traditional mentoring: 1:1 knowledge transfer
AI-assisted mentoring: 1:N execution scaling

**Result:** Strategic decisions implemented faster, allowing more strategic thinking

### Replicable Methodology

1. Start with real project (not tutorials)
2. AI executes, human strategizes
3. Regular assessments (capture growth)
4. Iterate on decisions (risk analysis)
5. Document process in real-time
6. Trust human intuition over AI analysis
